About Company

I started Kinect Audio Video in 2015 with an dream to be able to offer the complete solutions and installation that will make your ideas for your home or car reality. The company will offer both mobile design, installation/setup for both residential and commercial properties as well as a wide variety of automotive installations.

Kinect Audio Video works only with quality parts and materials from featured brands. I am happy to provide information and warranty about all products used in my installations. I am also proud to provide special offers on select parts for online purchasing.

Kinect Audio video staff are all fully certified , licensed and insured to cover all aspects of our untouchable design, installation and above all great customer service.

About Me

I’ve had a passion for electronics ever since I was young. I found the car bug before I was even able to drive.

Having worked in a multiple high end car audio installation shops I learned to a wide range of skills from performing basic installations to managing advanced design projects. I’ve managed several car installation shops including Alexis Mobile Hi-Fi and Future Shop in St.Catharines where I held a position of car audio multibay manager and lead installer.

I then became extremely interested in a home theater custom design which led me to become an installer for both Future Shop ConnectPro and Best Buy Geek squad. I’ve designed, managed and completed many residential and commercial home theater and audio-video installations for thousands of clients in Niagara area.
